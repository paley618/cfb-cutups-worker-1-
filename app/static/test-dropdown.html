How to See Step 5

  1. Go to the main page (/)
  2. Hard refresh your browser:

    • Mac: Cmd + Shift + R
  3. Scroll down to the CFBD section
  4. Check the "Use CFBD-guided cuts" checkbox (if visible)
  5. You should see ALL 5 steps listed, including "Step 5: Verify Game ID"
